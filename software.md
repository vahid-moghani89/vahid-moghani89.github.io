    <h3>LeaveOutKSS</h3>
    <p>
      LeaveOutKSS is an R implementation of the leave-out correction for variance-component
      estimation in two-way fixed-effects (AKM) models, following Kline, Saggio, and
      Sølvsten, <em>Leave-out Estimation of Variance Components</em> (<em>Econometrica</em>, 2020).
    </p>
    <p>
      The package is based on the authors’ original MATLAB package and mirrors its core
      functionality and workflow in R.
    </p>
